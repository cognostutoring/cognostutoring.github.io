How you can position your services

Built to present your tutoring offer in a more polished way

  • Subject reinforcement

    Support weekly coursework, homework review, and concept mastery with steady, structured tutoring sessions.

  • Test preparation

    Position this area for quizzes, unit tests, finals, and standardized exam preparation with focused review plans.

  • Consistent scheduling

    Communicate dependable session cadence, recurring support, and simple next-step scheduling for busy families.

  • Flexible session format

    Explain whether you offer virtual support, in-person tutoring*, or a hybrid model that fits different student needs.

  • Goal-based planning

    Show families that each tutoring plan can be aligned to grades, coursework, confidence-building, or long-term academic goals.

  • Parent Communication

    Parents are looped in on every session via a personalized document, and conferences are offered at your convenience.
